# Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 19, 2026

## Requesting deletion

Because this application is currently a closed test, users may request deletion of their data by sending a message to:

**+569 6812 4581**

## What to include

To help us identify the relevant records, please include:

- the phone number used to contact the WhatsApp test number
- the approximate date or dates of interaction
- any other details that help identify the conversation

## What we will do

After receiving a valid request, we will make a good-faith effort to:

- locate the relevant stored test records
- delete or anonymize the data associated with the requesting user
- confirm completion of the request when finished

## Limits

In some cases, limited information may need to be retained temporarily for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, or operational troubleshooting. If that applies, we will limit retention to what is reasonably necessary.

## Alternative contact

If you cannot send email, you may request deletion through another contact method made available by the test operator.
